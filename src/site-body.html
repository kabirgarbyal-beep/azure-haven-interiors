01 — Studio

A studio devoted to the discipline of stillness.

For seventeen years Maison Lumière has worked at the intersection of architecture, material research and the intimate scale of domestic life. Our practice is small by design — fewer than a dozen projects each year, each treated as a singular commission.

We collaborate with quiet patrons, principal architects and the finest ateliers in Europe and India: stone-cutters in Carrara, joiners in Jodhpur, lacquer specialists in Kyoto.

  • — Hospitality & boutique commercial
  • — Custom furniture & material direction

03 — Process

Four chapters. One quiet conversation.

  1. i.

    Discovery

    A private conversation at your residence. We listen — to how you live, host, retreat, collect.

  2. ii.

    Architecture of Intent

    A written brief, a material atlas and the first spatial diagrams. Nothing decorative — yet.

  3. iii.

    Atelier

    Custom joinery, lighting and stone are prototyped in our workshop. Samples travel to you.

  4. iv.

    Installation

    A single project lead is on site daily. We hand over a home that is fully styled and ready to live in.

07 — Enquiries

Questions we are often asked.

What is the typical project size you accept?

Residential commissions from 4,000 sq ft upward, and selected hospitality projects. We take on no more than ten engagements per year.

Do you work outside India?

Yes. Active projects in London, Dubai, Milan, Geneva, Singapore and New York. A senior lead is on site weekly.

How long does a typical residence take?

From first conversation to handover, twelve to eighteen months for a full-scope villa or penthouse.

How are fees structured?

A fixed design fee for the studio's intellectual work, plus a transparent procurement model. Shared in full at the second meeting.

Do you guarantee confidentiality?

Always. We sign mutual NDAs before discovery and publish work only with explicit consent.
